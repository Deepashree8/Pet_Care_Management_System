Puppies (0-6 Months)

Morning: 1/4 cup of high-quality puppy kibble mixed with a tablespoon of wet food.

Afternoon: 1/4 cup of kibble mixed with a small amount of boiled chicken.

Evening: 1/4 cup of kibble with a few bite-sized pieces of carrot or green beans.

Adult Dogs (1-7 Years)

Morning: 1/2 cup of high-quality adult dog food.

Evening: 1/2 cup of dog food mixed with vegetables.

Senior Dogs (7+ Years)

Morning: 1/2 cup of senior dog food.

Evening: 1/2 cup of food mixed with soft food for easier chewing.

Kittens (0-6 Months)

Morning: 1/4 cup of high-quality kitten food.

Afternoon: 1/4 cup of wet food.

Evening: 1/4 cup of kitten kibble.

Adult Cats (1-7 Years)

Morning: 1/2 cup of high-quality adult cat food.

Evening: 1/2 cup of wet food.

Senior Cats (7+ Years)

Morning: 1/2 cup of senior cat food.

Evening: 1/2 cup of food mixed with soft food for easier chewing.

Homemade Pet Food Options
For Dogs:
  • Chicken and Rice: Boiled chicken (boneless) mixed with boiled rice and vegetables (like carrots and peas).
  • Vegetable Porridge: Cooked vegetables (carrots, peas, pumpkin) mixed with rice and lentils.
  • Egg and Vegetable Mix: Scrambled eggs mixed with steamed vegetables.
For Cats:
  • Fish and Rice: Cooked fish (like mackerel or sardines) mixed with boiled rice.
  • Chicken Stew: Boiled chicken pieces with broth and some vegetables (avoid onions and garlic).
  • Egg Custard: Scrambled eggs or boiled eggs as a protein source.
